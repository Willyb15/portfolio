William's Story

William received his BBA from The University of Georgia and most recently was Operations Manager of The Snake River Brewing Company, Ski Instructor at Jackson Hole Mountain Resort, and River Guide at Mad River Boat Trips. William decided to switch gears and enter the field of web development. Currently he is enrolled in a 16 week program at Digital Crafts with curriculum covering full-stack web development. His initial skillset was focused around JavaScript, jQuery, HTML, and CSS. Since then he has embraced the backend by learning the LAMP and MEAN stacks. There is still much to be learned.
